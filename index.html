What is Telemedicine?

Telemedicine is not meant to replace traditional face to face examinations. Rather, it is meant to enhance veterinary care delivery by making it possible for veterinarians to evaluate, diagnose, and follow-up with their furry patients remotely by using technology. This approach has been quickly gaining adoption by both the human and animal healthcare industries over the past decade and is becoming a vital part of the American healthcare infrastructure.

Why now?

Telemedicine has been identified as a significant area to explore by the NAVC Veterinary Innovation Council.

70% of veterinarians polled reported that they believe telemedicine technology will strengthen VCPR.

83% of hospital executives plan to invest in telehealth systems and services to outpace competition and broaden their reach.
